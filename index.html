Pediatric Endocrine Panel Management Dashboard

Guideline-aligned panel triage for glycemic control, follow-up gaps, technology gaps, and screening gaps

Expected CSV format

patient_id,diabetes_type,last_a1c,prev_a1c,bmi,prev_bmi,days_since_last_visit,missed_visits,cgm_use,time_in_range,gmi,last_eye_exam_days,last_kidney_screen_days

How this prototype triages patients

  • Urgent review: very high A1c or markedly worsening control
  • Closer follow-up: above-target A1c, rising trend, or visit-gap concerns
  • Technology gap: not on CGM or missing CGM-informed data
  • Screening gap: overdue eye or kidney screening
  • Routine: stable control and no major care gaps
